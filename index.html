sabeeha's pani puri recipe

ingrediants

  • few potatos
  • flour
  • coriander leafs
  • water
  • chat masala
  • salt
  • pepper
  • mint leafs
  • chillies with chilli powder
  • peas

instructions

  1. first of all boil the potatoes
  2. next peel off the potatoes then smash the potatoes well
  3. then add peas,coriander leafs,salt,coriander powder,chilli power,pepper etc.
  4. after that mix it well
  5. the stuff is ready now we need to prepare puries
  6. take flour and pour the water into the flour and mix it well and do like chapaties and take one little glass prepare little puries and then boil that puries in the oil
  7. now prepare mint water
  8. take mint leafs,salt,chillies,ginger,coriander leafs etc.take it into mixy and graind it well..after that take that paste into one bowl,pour the water and add little bit of salt
  9. then mix it well
  10. finally our pani puri is ready to eat
